About Joanne

Joanne Raffle is an experienced piano teacher offering lessons to children and adults. Her philosophy centres on nurturing each student's love for music, encouraging creativity and building a solid foundation of technique and theory. Whether you're a beginner or returning to the instrument, Joanne will tailor lessons to your goals and pace.

Lessons Offered

A variety of lesson formats to suit your needs.

Children's Lessons

Creative and engaging lessons that combine games, songs and technique to spark a lifelong love of music.

Adult Lessons

Whether you're starting from scratch or returning after a break, Joanne will help you achieve your musical goals in a supportive environment.

Group Sessions

Small group lessons are a fun way to learn with friends or siblings while developing ensemble skills.

Why Learn with Joanne?

Every student is unique. Joanne blends structure with creativity to foster a lifelong love of music.

Personalized Instruction

Lessons are tailored to your goals, whether you’re preparing for exams, learning to read music or simply playing for fun.

Creative & Fun

Games, improvisation and composition keep lessons engaging and help develop a deeper connection to music.

Performance Opportunities

Students can participate in informal recitals and share their progress with family and friends when they’re ready.
